Our story

Luna Media is a team of advertising technology experts, with decades of experience across publishing, agency planning and brand marketing. Our results-driven, ever-growing, international team got together to make the complicated stuff behind digital media look easy. Together we’ve built industry-leading, machine-learning technology that helps hundreds of brand advertisers, agencies and mobile apps reach their marketing objectives while driving millions of dollars to digital publisher’s mobile apps, CTV apps and online websites. We’re here to help grow your business.
